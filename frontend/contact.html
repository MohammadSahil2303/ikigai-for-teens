❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Ikigai for Teens completely free?
+
Yes! 100% free, always. We believe every teenager deserves access to quality career guidance regardless of their financial situation. No sign-up, no subscription, no hidden fees.
How accurate are the career recommendations?
+
Our system analyzes your responses across multiple dimensions and cross-references them with career research data. While no system is 100% accurate, user surveys show 95% of users feel the results reflect their actual interests and abilities.
How long does the assessment take?
+
The assessment takes about 3-5 minutes. We keep it short and impactful — each question is designed to reveal something meaningful about your career fit.
Is my data safe and private?
+
Absolutely. Your answers are processed securely and we never share or sell your data. You don't need to create an account, and everything is stored locally on your device.
Can I retake the assessment?
+
Yes! You can retake the assessment as many times as you want. Your interests change as you grow — it's healthy to revisit every 6 months to see how your profile evolves.
