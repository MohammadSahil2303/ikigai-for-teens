📖 Understanding Ikigai

Your Sweet Spot Exists

In the Japanese island of Okinawa — home to the world's longest-living people — Ikigai (ee-key-guy) means "reason for being." It's the place where four elements come together:

❤️
Passion — What you Love
Activities that make you lose track of time. Things that excite and energize you.
💪
Profession — What You're Good At
Your natural talents and learned skills. What others recognize you for.
🌍
Mission — What the World Needs
Problems you care about solving. The impact you want to have on others.
💰
Vocation — What You Can Be Paid For
A career that not only fulfills you but also provides financial stability.
